# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Mahjong players and gift-buyers who care about the object as much as the game: people who host game nights, collect beautiful things, and would pass a set down. Secondary: the three founders themselves (studio.html is their private design tool) and press/stockists reading the brand book.

## Product Purpose

Gathered Mahjong is a boutique mahjong house founded by three friends (Kelly, Jill, Nha), selling heirloom tile sets painted from the original brushwork of artist Nha Vuu. The site's job is to earn VIP waitlist signups for Collection No. 001 (Modern Palms), a first pressing of a few hundred sets. Success = a visitor feels the object's quality before it exists, and leaves an email.

## Brand Personality

Warm, deliberate, small-on-purpose. Speaks "like a letter, not a landing page": short sentences, concrete words (table, friends, hands, ink). The art gets the credit; Nha's name appears wherever her work does. Quietly luxurious, never salesy.

## Anti-references

- Generic DTC luxury landing pages (gradient heroes, hype countdowns, scarcity-marketing tone).
- Mass-market game-store aesthetics; anything that reads "product SKU" instead of "painting become tile".
- AI gloss copy: seamless, curated, crafted with love, elevate. No em dashes. No stacked exclamation marks.

## Design Principles

1. **The art gets the credit.** Nha's brushwork is the hero; UI chrome stays quiet around it.
2. **Small is the point.** Limited runs are stated plainly, never dressed up as scarcity marketing.
3. **The tile is the design system.** Gallery Blue backing, bone face, brass keyline, italic G monogram: every surface echoes the physical tile.
4. **Like a letter, not a landing page.** Copy is short, warm, and concrete.
5. **Craft in the fine detail.** Optical alignment, keylines, and letter-spacing compensation are the brand; sloppy pixels contradict the product.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA contrast on text; visible focus states; `prefers-reduced-motion` honored on all animation; touch targets 44px on mobile. CJK characters set only in serif CJK faces, honoring the traditional tile forms.
