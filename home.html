Modern Palms

Our first tile collection, drawn from Nha's Modern Palms series: fronds in ink and gold over bone-white faces, backed in deep gallery blue. These are studies of the set to come; final artwork arrives with the fall.

The Gathered Mat

A table deserves dressing. Our launch mat is cut in the same deep gallery blue as the tile backs, edged in a fine brass keyline, with a cushioned surface that hushes the shuffle and flatters every wash.

One mat, one collection, made to live together.

  • Plush, low-glare playing surface in gallery blue
  • Rolls flat every time, with no creases and no curl
  • Brass keyline border echoing the tile engraving
